Quality: About This Data Set

Both these two datasets are collected by aware software. The first data is location of smart phone, including timestamp, altitude, longtitude and latitude information. The second data is battery condition of phones, including timestamp, battery level, and battery state(no charge, charging, full).

Is the data complete?

No, the data is not complete. For battery data, we lose some data when the software is not on. But I think it is not a big deal since the level of battery is continues and time period we lose is not very long, so for the general trend, we can infer what happened during the time that data losed.

Is the data coherent?

Yes, the data is coherent. All the conclusion we can get from the data is inaccordance to the real condition for me and my parent. It just match my expectation. Also, there is no conflict between those data.

Is the data correct?

I think the data is correct, about the location, data points that visualized are just where I went to during the week and the battery level and condition is also confirmed to the real condition.
