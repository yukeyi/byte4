Keyi Yu's Interactive Data Science Project Exploratory Analysis Byte

This is the Exploratory Analysis Byte of the Interactive Data Science class.

Question: What's the different between I and my parent's life.

To answer this question, I collect some mobile datas using aware and then visualized them and made my explanation. Here, I only took my mother into comparison for simplfy. First, I visualized the location data in a week. Shown on the Open StreetMap, I use the longtitude and latitude to locate one person and use different color to represent different altitude. Cooler color represents lower altitude and warmer color represent higher altitude. As we can see, my location distributed more concentrated, spent almost half of the time at school and half of the time at home(some time on the way for school or home). While for my mother, her location distributed more scattered, which make sense since she have more things to do expect work, like go shopping, go to my grandparents' home. Besides that, from the altitude data we can know that Pittsburgh is higher than Shanghai, and since I live in Squirral Hill, I need to climb mountain every day home, shown by obviously cool color on the left and warm color on the right. While for my mother, altitude has only a little varience, which means everyday she do not need to go up and dowm, most road are flat. To sum up, everyday my life is fixed at home or school, and need to climb mountain, while my mother need to go to more places and life is more complicated and all the roads are flat.

Ater that, I use another data gathered by aware - battery for smart phone to illustate. I visualized result in the following way, X coordinate represents time in a week and Y coordinate represents battery level of the phone, that is, how many battery left. And I use shallow color yellow to represent phone is not charged and dark color orange wo represent phone is charging. Red just means battery is in full condition. We can see that there are some time periods that we do not have data. I think that makes sense because sometimes we may forget to start aware software (most happens at night after we refresh the phone) so that it cannot gather data. From the figure we can see that my mother consume the battery more quickly and recharge it more frequently than me. I think it means that my mother play with the phone more often than me. This just in accordance with condition that she use her phone to watch online video and make phone call more frequently. In comparison, I spent more time with computer so less time with my phone. To sum up, as a CMU student, I seem more busy than my mother. One thing need to claim is that there is a difference between two phone's battery quality and capacity, which will make the conclusion weak. However, since two Phones are both iPhone 7 PLUS, I think this issue is not decisive.
